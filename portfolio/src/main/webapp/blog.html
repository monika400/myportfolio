My journey to yoga



During lockdown period i tried my hands on different things. Started with cooking food, making dresses, decorating walls ,etc. But i end up with yoga. One day i was just scrolling my phone i found some yoga poses, I tried them next morning and i enjoyed. Then i started practising different yoga. Sometime i do meditation at morning and the feeling is just awesome. I tried handstand, headstand, split but i failed. But i never left yoga and within 4-5 days i was able to do hand stand and all yoga i want to do. This is how journey begins. And truely said:
Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self - Bhagvad Gita
